Welcome!

Submitting an event permit to Business Affairs is simple and should take no longer than 10 minutes to complete.

Permits should be applied for at least two weeks prior to the date of the event.

Are you a Registered Student Organization? If you are, please use the Student Engagement Permit Request System.
